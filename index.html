Mapping

We Map the past events of landslides using various AI and non-AI based techniques to identify landslide hazards.

Understanding

We use numerical modelling and geo-spatial analysis as well as exploratory analysis to understand the physics of landslides

Predicting

We create state-of-the-art landslide models using AI and Statstics to predict the future landslide hazard.
